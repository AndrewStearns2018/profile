Three books you should read

Ada or Ardor

Van and Ada go on incestuous romps all over their home-planet of Anti-Terra and still make time for Gallo-Russian puns and digs at Maupassant. Certain readers will wonder if Nabokov inists on a specific Latin transliteraton for Ѣ.

Le Temps retrouvé

The narrator convalesces à la campagne during the Great War and goes back to Paris to find himself in a molly house before going to a matinée mondaine.

Rêveries d'un promeneur solitaire

Rouseau proto-romantically talks about plants and self-indulgently bitches about his persecution.
